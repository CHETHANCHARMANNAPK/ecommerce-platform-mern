Here is how to get the connection string:
1. Click the **Connect** button right under the green checkmark "Sample data loaded successfully".
2. A modal will pop up. Under "Connect to your application", click **Drivers**.
3. You will see a connection string that looks something like this under step 3:
   `mongodb+srv://<username>:<password>@cluster0.xxxx.mongodb.net/?retryWrites=true&w=majority`
4. Copy that exact string.
